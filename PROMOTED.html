Real Breda CF Promoted to Division 3!

Real Breda CF has secured a dramatic promotion to Division 3 following a high-intensity showdown that tested the team’s resilience, composure, and heart. The squad held their ground despite going down to nine men, sealing a 2-1 victory that will be remembered for seasons to come.

Goalkeeper Luiz Diaz was the hero of the night with a crucial penalty save late in the second half that preserved the lead and kept promotion hopes alive. The save sent shockwaves through the stadium and ignited belief throughout the team.

The breakthrough came through Remy Markovic, who opened the scoring with a precise finish after a fluid attacking move. Shortly after, Twinck doubled the lead with a confident strike that left the opposition keeper rooted.

However, the match wasn’t without controversy. Pimladen and Patrick both received red cards in a fiery second half, forcing Real Breda to defend with everything they had. Despite the pressure, the defense held strong, anchored by Luiz Diaz's brilliance in goal.

This unforgettable victory marks the beginning of a new chapter in Division 3. Real Breda CF will now look to carry this momentum forward and challenge for even greater honors.
